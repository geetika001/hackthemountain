What we do?

Patient to Patient Chat

Our platform ensures that patients going through same the same problems, talk with each other, share their ideas, feelings, struggles, maybe some tips to overcome the phase and support(morale boost) from each other.

Anonymous identity

Nowadays, not everyone is comfortable in sharing their problems with someone unknown. So, we bring the feature of keeping the identity hidden, ensuring comfortable chat.

Availability of mentors

We know that, sometimes it becomes difficult for the doctor to communicate with the patient. In this case, we have the feature of mentors, who have the knowledge about medical and can provide comfort to the patient whenever needed.
